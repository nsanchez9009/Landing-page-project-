The Fool is the fifth studio album by Bladee. All songs on the project are produced by Lusi, along with other producers like Loesoe, who appears multiple times on the album. The project features Lusi’s signature trap drums but many of the songs use pop and trance melodies. It was debuted along with a video for “The Fool Intro.”
